Online Product Catalogue

An Online Product Catalog is an excellent solution for a seller to showcase their list of products online to their end customers (product buyers).

Why?

This will save time of both end customers / buyers and sellers.

In ideal situation, the buyer would not know that a particular seller has a specific product or a unique design which he is looking for, unless he enters the shop.

With our Online Product Catalogue, the seller can showcase entire list of products (Category and Sub-Category wise) to their end customers. So that the buyer can select the product and walk into the shop to purchase. Online shopping cart option can also be provided, if needed.

As and when the seller has a new product, she/he can add to product details and the same will automatically start appearing in the product list screen.

Buyer can navigate via Category to Sub-Category to Product List to Product Details screen. Also search across the products by entering the search keywords or what they are looking for, in the search box.
