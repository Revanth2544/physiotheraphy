    How it Works

    Step 1 Initial Consultation:

    Conduct a thorough initial assessment including medical history, physical examination, and understanding patient goals. Use assessment tools and diagnostic tests as needed.

    Step 2 Personalized Treatment Plans:

    Develop a personalized treatment plan based on the initial assessment. Set specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals.

    Step 3 Patient Education:

    Educate patients about their condition, treatment options, and expected outcomes. Provide information on exercises, lifestyle modifications, and preventive measures.

    Step 4 Personalized Treatment Therapy Sessions:

    Administer treatments as per the individualized plan, using techniques such as manual therapy, exercise therapy, electrotherapy, etc. Monitor patient progress and adjust treatment plans as needed.

    Step 5 Patient Engagement:

    Encourage active patient participation in their recovery process. Provide home exercise programs and follow-up instructions.

    Step 6 Regular Follow-Up:

    Schedule regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and adjust treatment plans. Use follow-up sessions to reinforce patient education and engagement.

    Step 7 Outcome Measurement:

    Use standardized outcome measures to evaluate the effectiveness of treatments. Collect patient feedback and satisfaction surveys to improve services.
